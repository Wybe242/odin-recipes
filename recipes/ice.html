Ice

Ice: the unsung hero of the culinary world, a crystalline marvel forged in the frozen depths of your freezer. Elegant in its simplicity, it elevates any beverage from lukewarm regret to frosty perfection.

Recipe

Prep Time: 5 seconds

Cook Time: 4 hours (unless your freezer has performance anxiety)

Ingridents

  • 1 cup water (organic if you're fancy)
  • 2 tablespoons water (additional as needed)

Instructions

  1. Pour water into an ice cube tray. Take a moment to feel like a culinary genius.
  2. Carefully walk to the freezer like you're carrying a bomb in a movie.
  3. Place tray in freezer. Close door. Whisper words of encouragement.
  4. Wait 4 hours or until frozen solid—just like your ex’s heart.
  5. Pop out cubes and serve in drinks, or throw at friends for a refreshing surprise.

Pro tip: Don't try to flip them like pancakes. They are not pancakes.
